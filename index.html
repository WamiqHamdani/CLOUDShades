About the Villa

CLOUD Shades Villa —
Kashmir's Finest Holiday Home

Cloud Shades is a premium vacation home/villa property located in Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir, India. A popular holiday home option for visitors to the region, it is consistently listed and top-rated across all major booking platforms.

The villa is perfectly situated in the Sudarbal area, approximately 4–6 km from the iconic Hazratbal Mosque, 13 miles from Shankaracharya Mandir, and 15 km from Pari Mahal — making it an ideal base for exploring Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake, and all of Srinagar's celebrated landmarks.
